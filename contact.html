Contact us

If you want to contact us, you can do so here. Below are some common reasons you many want to contact us. If your need falls under one of those, please use the listed means of contact to ensure your message gets to the right place.

Common reasons for contacting SOUP

  • Learn when upcoming events will be held

If you want to get information on our various upcoming events, go to this page, or email dinner@bhsoup.org for updates on dinner dates. You can also email updates@bhsoup.org to recieve a periodic newsletter containing this and other information.

  • Learn more about leadership

To learn about how BH SOUP's leadership works, get in contact with us at leadership@bhsoup.org!

  • Ask a general question about SOUP

If you have a general question about SOUP, you can send it to contact@bhsoup.org. We'll get someone to answer it for you as quickly as possible. Don't be afraid to ask whatever you need to; it'll help us develop a much needed FAQ for our About page.

  • Apply to present at an upcoming meeting

If you're part of another organization or represent them in some way, we'd love to have you at SOUP! If you fill out the google form here , we'll consider your organization and possibly contact you to present at an upcoming SOUP dinner. In this relatively early stage in the progress of our organization, most applicants are accepted, and all are encouraged to apply. It won't cost you anything to present, and you stand to make hundreds of dollars for your charity. If you're at all interested, please fill out the survey

  • Sponsor BH SOUP

If you feel that your company or organization would be interested in sponsoring SOUP, wether by providing food or money, we'd be incredibly thankful for your support. Please contact us at sponsor@bhsoup.org to ensure your offer is seen in a timely manner. Thank you for any support you might be able to provide, and don't worry that your contributions will be used for anything but the donations to the winning charities; we don't keep any money for ourselves, we just pass it on to others who can use it to change the world for the better
